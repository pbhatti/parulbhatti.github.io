Tablehero

At Tablehero, I was part of the three-member design & product team, responsible for designing the business management toolkit for mom-and-pop restaurants in the US. My role encompassed conceptualizing the product from the ground up, defining user workflows, building and testing concepts using low- and high-fidelity prototypes, and delivering pixel-perfect front-end development for select workflows.

Reservations · ipad app

The brief was to build a simple reservation & waitlist management app which helps restauranter manage their diners better. We kept it lightweight with no feature bloat. The focus was on solving for the core user needs first, i.e., a simple onboarding, restaurant set-up, table management & guest book that even the most low-tech owners could use.


Target customers

The target customers were the mom and pop establishments who manages a full-service dining room of any size using pen-and-paper and don’t want to be held hostage by expensive & complicated solutions available in the market like Yelp and OpenTable.

Sites · web app

The objective was to enable small business owners to build their website as fast as possible and keep it ‘very beginner-friendly’. The solution we arrived at was an automated website builder that pulls content either from the business’s existing website or any other online presence into a mobile-friendly & responsive template in just one click!


Target customers

The target customers were small local business owners. To start with, we targeted mom & pop restaurants who are resistive of high budget, clunky website builder with complex editors that required heavy duty & time consuming content uploads.

‘Rise’ - Template Design

Rise is a fashion-forward, ultra-clean template, designed to give the restaurant website a modern and approachable feel. I used primary & bold hues on actionable & modern sans-serif typeface, along with a sleek & compact theme to put the content front and center.


Sites offered more than 20 beautiful, responsive & free website templates, all based on different themes, designed by various designers.

Orders · web app

To enable restaurant owners to take online takeout orders on their websites. We designed a readily insertable, fully responsive orderable menu widget. It comes in different templates/colors, giving restaurant owner flexibility to select & match it to their existing website theme. The widget comes with a orders & revenue management dashboard.


Target customers

The target customers were restaurant owners who wanted to get rid of pdf menus on their website or were stuck with a non-responsive webpage; as a result, losing on the untapped mobile customer base.
